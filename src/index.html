What is Bitrix24?

Bitrix24 is a complete suite of social collaboration, communication and management tools for your team.

4+ millions
organizations are already using Bitrix24

Main features

Free collaboration suite with 35+ tools

Social network

An internal social network lets you collaborate easier, faster and more efficiently. Get instant feedback, share ideas, create workgroups and engage your employees.

Bitrix24.Drive

Share files and folders easily with your co-workers, access your data on any device and get rid of your old file server, because you no longer need it.

Telephony

Make phone calls to your clients and co-workers from your portal with one click. Log and record phone calls directly inside the CRM.

Tasks and projects

From personal ToDos and simple tasks to complex projects — Bitrix24 helps delegate and coordinate, making sure the job is done properly and on time.

Calendar and Planing

Invite your colleagues to a meeting, schedule appointments with clients directly from the CRM, create private and shared calendars for yourself, workgroups, or entire company.

HR

Human resources management system comes with an employee directory, absence chart, self service portal, time management, work reports and 20+ other HR tools.

Group chat and video

Your business can’t wait, so use real time communication tools from videoconferencing to group chat available in Bitrix24 absolutely free.

Email

If you work with email daily, you are sure to appreciate ability to access it directly from your Bitrix24 account.

Mobile

It works on your iPhone, iPad or Android too. You can take your Bitrix24 account with your phone or tablet and it will always be only one tap away.

Document management

Secure online document storage, online multi-user editing and custom document approval workflows are just a few advantages of Bitrix24’s document management system.

Sales

Sell more with Bitrix24’s CRM and sales team management. From customer database to email marketing and automatic lead assignment — it’s all there.
